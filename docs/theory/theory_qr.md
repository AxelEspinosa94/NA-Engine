# QR Decomposition — Theory

QR factorization decomposes:

\[
A = QR
\]

where:

- \( Q \) is orthogonal  
- \( R \) is upper triangular  

---

# 1. Solving a System

1. Compute \( y = Q^T b \)  
2. Solve \( Rx = y \)

---

# 2. Applications

- Least squares  
- Eigenvalue algorithms  
- Numerical stability
