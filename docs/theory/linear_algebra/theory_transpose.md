# Matrix Transpose — Theory

The transpose of a matrix $A$ is obtained by flipping rows and columns:

$$
A^T_{ij} = A_{ji}
$$

---

# 1. Properties

- $(A^T)^T = A$  
- $(AB)^T = B^T A^T$  
- $(A^{-1})^T = (A^T)^{-1}$

---

# 2. Conceptual Example

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
4 & 3 \\
6 & 3
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
A^T = \begin{pmatrix}
4 & 6 \\
3 & 3
\end{pmatrix}
$$
