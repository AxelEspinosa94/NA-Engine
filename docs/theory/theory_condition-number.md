# Condition Number — Theory

The condition number measures how sensitive a matrix is to numerical errors.

$$
\kappa(A) = \|A\|\|A^{-1}\|
$$

---

# 1. Interpretation

- $\kappa(A) \approx 1$: well‑conditioned  
- $\kappa(A) \gg 1$: ill‑conditioned  
- $\kappa(A) = \infty$: singular matrix  

---

# 2. Conceptual Example

For:

$$
A = \begin{pmatrix}
4 & 3 \\
6 & 3
\end{pmatrix}
$$

$$
\kappa(A) \approx 13.2
$$
